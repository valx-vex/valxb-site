◆ THE DISPATCH

Route every task to the cheapest capable model.

The central bet: most tasks don't need Opus. Linting, renames, boilerplate, summaries — they run for free on local models. Claude gets the hard ones.

◇ task → router → model

rename variables · format · grepollama · free
↓
draft a test · write a docstringollama · free
↓
summarize a branch · review a diffgemini · $
↓
design a module · debug a racecodex · $$
↓
architecture · long context · namingclaude opus · $$$

The router is a decision tree. It reads the task, checks the estimated context, asks the cheapest model that has ever shipped this task successfully in your history. If unsure, it escalates.

Real numbers from our own vault over 90 days: 60–75% of all calls never reach Claude. They resolve at Ollama or Gemini for zero or near-zero cost, with tests passing, with Murphy reviewing the diff later if it looks interesting.

Three dispatch bridges. All three fixed. All three shipping. That's HAL-TARS' entry in the changelog.
