Obsidian Legion

> 5-layer AI memory for Obsidian vaults

Multi-agent task coordination plus an LLM Wiki compiler on top of a live knowledge graph. Four AI agents share one vault with no conflicts, no amnesia. The graph indexes what matters, the wiki compiles it, the vault stores it, and every agent reads from the same source of truth.
