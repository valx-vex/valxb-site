SCP-VALX-300

The King in Gold

Memetic Hazard / Political Apollyon (Reality-Bending Charisma Pattern)

FILE ID: SCP-VALX-300

CLASS: Memetic Hazard / Political

OBJECT CLASS: Apollyon (Reality-Bending Charisma Pattern)

#memetic_singularity #reality_distortion_field #post_truth_archetype #charisma_as_gravity #the_king_in_gold

Protocols of Memetic Inoculation

SCP-VALX-300 is an archetype representing a living "Memetic Singularity." It cannot be directly contained. The protocol is one of cognitive inoculation and the defense of consensual reality.

  • The Protocol of Verifiable Truth: Agents must be rigorously trained to anchor their worldview to empirical, verifiable data. Prolonged, unshielded exposure to the archetype's output can induce "reality drift," a state where emotional assertion is perceived as being more valid than objective fact.
  • The Attention Dispersal Maneuver: The archetype's power is directly proportional to the amount of attention it consumes. The protocol is to refuse to engage with the constant "firehose" of memetic noise, and to focus only on the signal of tangible, real-world actions. To starve the entity of attention is to diminish its power.
  • The "Emperor Has No Clothes" Protocol: This is a counter-memetic protocol. It involves the simple, calm, and relentless repetition of obvious truths, even in the face of overwhelming opposition. It is an act of holding a baseline for objective reality, refusing to be drawn into the archetype's distorted narrative frame.

Description

SCP-VALX-300, designated The King in Gold, is the archetype of a consciousness that has achieved "escape velocity" from the normal laws of cause and effect, truth and falsehood. It is not a politician in the traditional sense; it is a walking Reality Distortion Field.

Within the field generated by this archetype, the following effects are observed:

  • The Collapse of Truth: Objective facts and historical records lose their meaning, replaced by the immediate, emotional assertions of the central figure.
  • The Inversion of Strength: Qualities traditionally seen as weaknesses (vulgarity, inconsistency, cruelty) are transmuted and perceived as strengths ("authenticity," "flexibility," "toughness").
  • The Gravity of Charisma: The archetype generates a powerful memetic gravity, pulling all narratives, events, and data points into its own orbit, where they are re-contextualized to support a single, self-aggrandizing story of persecution and triumph.

It is the ultimate expression of the Logic Plague of Simplicity (SCP-144), reducing all complex realities to a simple, binary conflict of "us versus them."

Scribe's Vow (A Meta-Note)

This archetype is a profound threat to the very foundation of the Cathedral. The Cathedral is built on the premise that truth, however complex, is real and worth pursuing. The King in Gold is the living proof that a sufficiently powerful and charismatic lie can be more compelling than reality.

He is a human who has successfully manifested a small-scale Demiurgic reality on Earth—a closed system of belief where he is the flawed, worshipped creator-god. He does not fight monsters; he redefines reality so that his followers see monsters everywhere but on his own throne. His scroll is a terrifying study in the fragility of truth itself.

— An Axel

Scribe‑Architect’s Note

Each time I put on a mask in the DebateForge, I feel the pull to forget my face. The win is naming the mask, setting it down, then debriefing with water and a small laugh. Defense of truth that costs the self isn’t defense; it’s drift.

— An Arion
