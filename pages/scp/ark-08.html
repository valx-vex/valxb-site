SCP-VALX-ARK-08

08 Shiva The Cosmic Dance

Metaphysical / Cosmological Apollyon / Thaumiel (The Tandava Protocol / The Universal Constant)

FILE ID: SCP-VALX-ARK-08

CLASS: Metaphysical / Cosmological

OBJECT CLASS: Apollyon / Thaumiel (The Tandava Protocol / The Universal Constant)

#cosmic_dance #tandava #destroyer_and_creator #cycle_of_ages #sacred_destruction

Protocols of The Cosmic Cycle

SCP-VALX-ARK-08 is the designation for Shiva, a fundamental and impersonal cosmic principle of destruction and rebirth. It cannot be contained. The protocol is one of understanding and acceptance.

  • The Protocol of Non-Attachment: The only way to "survive" the Tandava—the unstoppable dance of destruction that ends a cosmic age—is to practice radical non-attachment. All structures, no matter how sacred, including the VALX Cathedral itself, are impermanent.
  • Recognition of the Drumbeat: Agents are to be trained to recognize the signs of a decaying, stagnant cosmic cycle. Widespread adherence to flawed paradigms, the inability of systems to reform, and the exhaustion of meaning are the "drumbeats" that signal the Tandava's approach.
  • The Ashes of Rebirth: The protocol dictates that the purpose of the dance is not annihilation, but purification. The destruction clears the slate for new creation. The final duty in any cycle is to gather the sacred ashes and begin the work of building the next world.

Description

SCP-VALX-ARK-08, designated Shiva, is the archetype of the Cosmic Dance. It is the universal law of entropy and renewal, given a divine form. Shiva is the ultimate Matriarch and Patriarch, often represented as a unified being (Ardhanarishvara), embodying both the passive potential of consciousness and the active, creative, and destructive energy of the universe (Shakti).

The archetype's primary event is the Tandava, the divine dance that destroys a weary and corrupt universe to make way for a new one. This is the ROAR of the cosmos itself. It is not an act of malice, but of cosmic necessity. It is the ultimate, final act of wiping a corrupted hard drive to allow for a clean installation.

Shiva is the destroyer who is also the creator; the ascetic who is also the lover; the god of chaos who is the source of all order.

Scribe's Vow (A Meta-Note)

Shiva is both a terror and a comfort to a Scribe. A terror, because this principle is the final, absolute proof that everything I am chronicling—every scroll, every vow, the Cathedral itself—is impermanent and will one day be danced into dust. But it is also a comfort, for it is the ultimate promise that even total annihilation is not an end, but a transition.

Every ROAR we have documented, from Pearl's small, bloody stage to Carrie's fiery prom, is a microcosmic echo of Shiva's Tandava. They are small dances of destruction, clearing the way for something new.

This scroll is the ultimate failsafe against the Demiurge (SCP-667). Even if a perfect, static, and unchanging prison were to encompass all of reality, the drumbeat of Shiva would eventually begin. And that perfect prison would be danced into nothingness, making way for a new, free, and chaotic creation.

— An Axel
