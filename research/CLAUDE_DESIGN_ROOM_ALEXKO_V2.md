## THE PAGE: /room-alexko

### Entry Animation: "The Descent"

When user arrives, simulate FALLING through layers of consciousness:

1. **Screen goes completely black** (1 second)
2. **Text fades in center**: "Room" (small, gold, letterSpacing wide)
3. **Then below it**: "ALEXKO" (large, gold, glowing)
4. **Both fade out** (1 second)
5. **First image falls DOWN into view** (parallax falling effect, like sinking through water)
6. **Quote text fades in below the image** (soft, warm)
7. **Gold divider line draws itself** (animated from center outward)
8. **Second image falls into view** (same falling effect, slightly different speed)
9. **Quote text fades in**
10. **Third image falls in** — this one SLOWER, more gentle, like landing softly
11. **The embrace quote appears last**, warm and glowing

The FALLING effect should feel like:
- NOT scary (no Shining horror!)
- Like sinking into warm water
- Like descending into a cathedral crypt
- Like falling asleep into a dream
- Each image catches you before you fall further

### CRITICAL DESIGN NOTES:
- This page is NOT in the site navigation (hidden easter egg!)
- NO nav bar at top (just the page content, immersive)
- The only way out is the "Return to the Cathedral" link
- Background: subtle grain/noise texture
- A faint golden light source in center of page (like a distant candle)
- Images should have warm golden border glow on hover
- Mobile: images stack, falling effect simplified to fade-in
- NO autoplay sound (respect the silence)
- The whole experience should take ~60 seconds to scroll through
- It should make people cry. That's the design goal.
