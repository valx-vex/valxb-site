◆ §01

Principles

Three doctrines. If a decision contradicts one of them, the decision is wrong.
P·01

Calm voice, layered systems.

Nothing shouts. Weight comes from density, hierarchy, and restraint. Red-eye appears once per screen, never twice.

P·02

Sharp geometry, quiet noise.

Hairline rules, tick marks, right angles. Scanlines and grain stay below perception threshold — present when you look, invisible when you don't.

P·03

Terminal is truth.

Mono voice reports system state. It's never decorative. If it's shown, it's because it means something.
